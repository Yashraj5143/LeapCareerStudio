Why I Started Leap

Not a consultancy.
A mentorship.

I remember how confusing this process felt — too many options, no clear direction, and constant pressure to make the "right" decision. Everyone had an opinion. No one had the time.

Leap started from that place.

I wanted to create something more personal — where students aren't just guided through applications, but actually understand their choices. Where clarity matters more than credentials.

Today, I work closely with students one-on-one to help them move forward with intention and confidence — not just get into a university, but truly find the right path for who they are.

Renna Sadarangani
Founder, Leap Career Studio
